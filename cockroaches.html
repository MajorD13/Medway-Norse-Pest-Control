About Cockroach Control in Medway

Medway Norse Pest Control provides professional cockroach control services across all Medway towns and the surrounding areas of Kent. Our RSPH Level 2 qualified technicians are fully accredited and experienced in identifying, treating and preventing infestations in both residential and commercial properties.

As Medway Council's exclusive approved pest control provider, we offer the highest standard of professional pest management available in the area. All treatments are carried out in accordance with BPCA and CEPA guidelines using professional-grade products.

Our Treatment Approach

Gel bait programme targeting harbourage areas. Essential for food businesses and healthcare settings. Our technicians will carry out a thorough assessment of the affected areas before treatment begins, explain the process clearly, and provide you with a written service report following every visit. Where additional preparation is required, we will advise you in advance.
