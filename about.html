Medway Norse Pest Control: Your Local Experts, Protecting Our Community

At Medway Norse Pest Control, we're more than just a pest control company - we're your neighbours. While our roots are firmly planted in Medway, our service extends throughout Kent. Our team is made up of dedicated, local pest control technicians who live and work within the communities we serve. We have a genuine passion for Kent, and a deep understanding of the unique pest challenges faced by residents and businesses across the county, from the coastal towns to the rural heartlands. With over 60 years of combined experience in the pest control industry, our team holds RSPH Level 2 Award in Pest Management qualifications, demonstrating our commitment to the highest standards of professionalism and expertise. But beyond the qualifications, it's our in-depth local knowledge that truly sets us apart. We know the common pests that plague Kent homes and businesses, their habits, and the most effective ways to eliminate them and prevent their return. We've built upon our deep understanding of Medway to cover all areas of Kent. Each of our technicians brings their area of expertise to the table, meaning there's no pest problem too big, too small, or too unusual for us to handle. Whether it's a persistent rodent issue, a troublesome insect infestation, or the need for discreet and effective bird-proofing, we have a specialist ready to provide a tailored solution. We pride ourselves on being a one-stop shop for all your pest control needs across Kent.
