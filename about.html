Medway Norse Pest Control: Your Local Experts, Protecting Our Community

At Medway Norse Pest Control, we're more than just a pest control company - we're your neighbours. Our team is made up of dedicated, local pest control technicians who live and breathe Medway. We have a genuine passion for our area and a deep understanding of the unique pest challenges faced by our community. With over 60 years of combined experience in the pest control industry, our team holds RSPH Level 2 Award in Pest Management qualification, demonstrating our commitment to the highest standards of professionalism and expertise. But beyond the qualifications, it's our in-depth local knowledge that truly sets us apart. We know the common pests that plague Medway homes and businesses, their habits, and the most effective ways to eliminate them and prevent their return. Each of our technicians brings their own area of expertise to the table, meaning there's no pest problem too big, too small, or too unusual for us to handle. Whether it's a persistent rodent issue, a troublesome insect infestation, or the need for discreet and effective bird proofing, we have a specialist ready to provide a tailored solution. We pride ourselves on being a one-stop shop for all your pest control needs.
