Who We Are

Medway's Most Trusted Pest Control Team

At Medway Norse Pest Control, we're more than just a pest control company — we're your neighbours. Our team of dedicated, local technicians live and work within the communities we serve. We have a genuine passion for Medway and Kent, and a deep understanding of the unique pest challenges faced by residents and businesses across the area.

With over 60 years of combined experience, every member of our team holds RSPH Level 2 Award in Pest Management qualifications — the industry benchmark. But beyond the qualifications, it's our local knowledge that truly sets us apart. We know the common pests that affect Kent properties, their habits, and the most effective ways to eliminate them.

We're a proud part of the Norse Group — one of the UK's largest and most respected facilities management companies — giving us access to extensive resources, industry-leading training and a network of expertise.
