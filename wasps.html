About Wasp Nest Removal in Medway

Medway Norse Pest Control provides professional wasp nest removal services across all Medway towns and the surrounding areas of Kent. Our RSPH Level 2 qualified technicians are fully accredited and experienced in identifying, treating and preventing infestations in both residential and commercial properties.

As Medway Council's exclusive approved pest control provider, we offer the highest standard of professional pest management available in the area. All treatments are carried out in accordance with BPCA and CEPA guidelines using professional-grade products.
